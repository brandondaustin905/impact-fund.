Canadian Cheer Hub Impact Fund

Cheer is expensive. Cost, not talent, is the most common reason an athlete in this country stops competing. So we took it out of our own revenue instead of asking you for more.

$3 of $15 Every Premium membership is $15/month. Three dollars of it is set aside before we pay ourselves anything.

It is a flat amount off the sticker price, not a share of whatever's left at the end of the month. It happens automatically on every renewal, for every member, whether or not anyone is watching.

Where it goes

🥇 Athlete ScholarshipsRegistration fees and gear
🏟️ Facility GrantsMats and safety equipment
✈️ Team SponsorshipsTravel to competitions
💪 Skill DevelopmentClinics and privates

How you check us

Three numbers, updated every month: how many members are contributing, how much has been set aside in total, and how much has actually been granted back out to athletes and gyms.

Fund money sits in its own account, separate from the money that runs the business. A grant only counts once it has left that account.
